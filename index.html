Mental Health

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. Mental health affects how we sct, feel, and think. It determines how we handle stress, make healthy choices, and relate to others. Mental Health important at every stage of your life.

Climate Change

Climate change is the long term shift in weather and temperature patterns. It is a big threat to humanity, and it is impacting the health of people through air pollution, forced displacement, extreme weather events, disease, pressures on mental health, and poor nutrition in places where people cannot gow/find sufficient food which can lead to hunger.

Here are orginizations that help:

  • Climate Action Network
  • Climate Group
  • Friends of the Earth International
